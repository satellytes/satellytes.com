# Leadbox
Give the user for further actions. This can either be a contact to approach or a destination to navigate to. Never use both.


<Canvas withSource='open'>
  <Story name="Regular">
    <Leadbox
      illustration={'space_shuttle_043'}
      title="Haben Sie Interesse bekommen?"
      contact={{
        email: "example@satellytes.com",
        title: "Gholam Abdol, Geschäftsführer",
        headline: "Projektanfrage"
      }}/>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

<ArgsTable of={Leadbox}/>

## Show a link
By giving a `link` object with `title` and `href` you can offer a button beneath the illustrations for the user's next action.

<Canvas withSource='open'>
  <Story name="With Link" >
    <Leadbox
      illustration={'blimp_001'}
      title="Haben Sie Interesse bekommen?"
      link={{
        title: "Karriere",
        href: 'http://www.example.com'
      }}/>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Show a contact
By giving a `contact` object with `email`, `title` & `headline` you can display a contact person to reach out for the user's next action.

<Canvas withSource='open'>
  <Story name="With Contact" >
    <Leadbox
      illustration={'alien_002'}
      title="Haben Sie Interesse bekommen?"
      contact={{
        email: "hello@example.com",
        title: "John Smith, CEO",
        headline: "Projektanfrage"
      }}/>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
